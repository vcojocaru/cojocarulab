### Structural dynamics in chromatin unraveling by pioneer transcription factors
**Biophysical Reviews**

Together with our collaborators in Utrecht, we have just published an article reviewing the mechanisms by which transcription factors unravel chromatin. 

The review comes with a different twist on the topic, highlighting the role of molecular dynamics simulations and nuclear magnetic resonance spectroscopy.
